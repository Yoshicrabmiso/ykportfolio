About Myself

Hello, こんにちは、侬好！I’m Yoshio, a UX designer with a rich blend of cultural and professional experiences. As a multilingual professional with Japanese and Chinese heritage, I bring a unique perspective to my design work, utilizing diverse cultural insights with a user-centered approach. With three years of experience in UX design, I've honed my skills in product development of various stages such as UI design for Web3 services, web app development for Fintech, and freelance UX research for Telecom, allowing me to gain a comprehensive understanding of both design and technical aspects. I primarily use Figma for visual mockups and FlutterFlow for further app development. The combination of these two tools has shaped my way of delivering product into the market: committing to understand user needs (within Japan, China) and delivering solutions that enhance their overall experience within short periods of time.

When I’m not designing, I enjoy exploring new design trends and techniques, and engaging with the broader design community. I’m excited about the opportunity to collaborate on innovative projects and create exceptional user experiences. Let’s connect and build something great together!
