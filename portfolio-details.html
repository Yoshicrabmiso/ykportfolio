ANAGranWhale / NFT Marketplace

Japanese Airline (ANA) brings a virtual traving experience to the world. ANAGranwhale/NFT Marketplace take you to explore the beauty of Japan and purchase items to create your own virtual world.

  • Client: ANA
  • Tool: Figma, Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop Unreal Engine
  • Span: 2 years

My role

  • ANAGranwhale: Building design system for the app, bringing png data from Figma to Unreal Engine which is where the app is made.
  • NFT Marketplace: In this project, I served as the design lead for “NFT Marketplace”, handling design tasks ranging from information architecture to designing each UI element. I was also responsible for researching current NFT market such as how users purchase items.

Background

During the COVID pandemic, ANA, a Japanese airline, aimed to offer customers a virtual travel experience. To fulfill this goal, We developed "ANAGranwhale," a metaverse platform allowing players to explore Japanese landmarks in virtual reality. This not only addresses the issue of ANA frequent flyers being unable to utilize their travel miles during real time traveling, but also serves as a platform for discovering places to visit in Japan post-pandemic. While developing ANAGranwhale, we encountered a problem with cryptocurrency item purchases in the game. As a solution, we created a separate ecommerce site called "ANAGranWhale NFT Marketplace" for item transactions.

ANA GranWhale Challenges

  • Creating UI elements that matches the app concept.
  • Figuring out the positioning of UI elements inside the game.
  • Span: 2 years

ANA GranWhale Solutions

  • The design team decided using white for main buttons, yellow for action buttons, and blue for secondary.

NFT Marketplace Challenges

  • User wants to purchase in store items other than in-game currency (Grand Coin).
  • User wants to import his or her own customizable items which are not available in ANAGranWhale.
  • Due to ANAGranWhale being a heavy app, users are frustrated with purchasing speed.

NFT Marketplace solutions

  • Import cryptocurrency with opportunity for users to purchase NFT items that are tied to ANAGranwhale.
  • Changing purchasing flow from in-game purchase to click-to-browser purchase
